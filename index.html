About Us

New Homes

We are working closely with clients and architects to move the project forward. We can fulfil a range of requests, from garages and refurbishments to a complete new home.

Commercial Buildings

Our scope of architecture does extend to commercial buildings. We are capable to deliver small to medium-sized projects. Building small office buildings, restaurants, extensions to premises or refurbishments.

Sustainability

We are committed to our corporate social responsibility policies and our company aims to provide the most sustainable materials and solutions to our customers. By working together with an external partner we are able to build zero emission and passive homes.

Modern Services

Upon request, our 3D & VR artist contractor will deliver cutting edge simulation of any internal of external design, so our customers can experience the architectural design and planning even before the construction starts!
